About me

A web development journey

Back in 2020, I decided to try customizing a WordPress theme for the website of the NGO I am volunteering at. It was a challenge, despite it not being my first attempt at customizing digital products. Mistakes were made, of course, but working on the website sparked an interest to know more.

Since developing this passion, I found myself easing into this new path. I started by delving deeper into User Experience and Interface Design and eventually completed an intensive Full Stack Web Development program.

I have worked on several projects using various technologies and tools. I began with my portfolio, where I mastered HTML and CSS, then moved on to more complex projects involving JavaScript. I have since progressed to developing web apps using React and Angular.

Currently, I am mostly focusing on improving my portfolio website and continuing to work on the WordPress website for Perspektiven für Kinder im Gallus, where I volunteer.

As a side project, I am in the initial stages of planning a Progressive Web App using the MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js and Node.js) stack to enhance my skills. The app will serve as a platform for entities to register kids' events and for users to discover and book these events.
