About

Web Development Journey

Back in 2020, I decided to try customizing a WordPress theme for the website of the NGO I was volunteering at. It was a challenge, despite it not being my first attempt at customizing digital products. Mistakes were made, of course, but working on the website sparked an interest to know more.

Since developing this passion, I found myself easing into this new path. I started by delving deeper into User Experience and Interface Design and eventually completed an intensive Full Stack Web Development program. I have worked on several projects using various technologies and tools, starting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and progressing to developing web apps in React and Angular. Check below my education and experiences in this field thus far.

Currently, I am mostly focusing on improving my portfolio website. Additionally, I am developing a web app with React to consolidate my knowledge, and I am continuing to work on the WordPress website for Perspektiven für Kinder im Gallus, where I volunteer.

Besides web development, I love planning the next town escape with my family, diving into a good book, crafting with kids, or watercolor painting.
